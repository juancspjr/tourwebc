Privacy Policy

At Rio Trip Vibes, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Request information about our tours
  • Make a reservation
  • Contact us via WhatsApp or email
  • Complete forms on our website

This information may include: name, email, phone number, nationality, and travel preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm your reservations
  • Communicate with you about your tours
  • Send relevant information about your trip
  • Improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Sharing Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data when necessary to provide our services (for example, with transportation or accommodation providers you have booked).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
