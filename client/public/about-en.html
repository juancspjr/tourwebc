About Rio Trip Vibes

Rio Trip Vibes is a travel agency specializing in personalized tours in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Founded with the mission of offering authentic and memorable tourist experiences, we have become a reference for travelers from around the world.

Our History

Rio Trip Vibes was born from a passion for sharing the beauty and culture of Rio de Janeiro with the world. Since 2019, we have guided over 1,000 satisfied travelers through unique experiences ranging from the iconic Christ the Redeemer to vibrant favelas and paradise beaches.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform every trip into an unforgettable experience. We believe tourism should be authentic, safe, and enriching. That's why all our tours are led by certified professionals who know every corner of the marvelous city.

Meet the Founder

Brian - CEO and Founder of Rio Trip Vibes

Brian is a certified tour guide with over 5 years of experience in Rio de Janeiro tourism. His passion for the city and commitment to service excellence led him to found Rio Trip Vibes.

With fluency in Spanish, English, Portuguese, French, and Italian, Brian and his team of bilingual guides ensure seamless communication with travelers from around the world.
